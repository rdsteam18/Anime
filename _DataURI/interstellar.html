Interstellar

92% liked this film 7 November 2014 R 2h 49m • Action, Thriller, Crime
Action Thriller Crime

When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.

Storyline

In the future, Earth is facing environmental collapse. Dust storms, crop failures, and dwindling oxygen levels threaten humanity’s survival. Cooper, a former NASA pilot turned farmer, lives with his daughter Murph and son Tom. Murph believes a mysterious force is trying to communicate with them through gravity. Following clues, Cooper and Murph discover a secret NASA base, where Professor Brand reveals that a wormhole near Saturn has opened a path to another galaxy. NASA believes this wormhole was placed by unknown beings, offering a chance to find a new habitable planet.
